Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients:

  • 1 bar of dark chocolate (150g)
  • 6 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar

How to do:

  1. Cut the dark chocolate bar in little pieces and melt it in the oven,
  2. While the cholate is melting, get six eggs and separate the white and yolk in two different containers,
  3. Beat the egg white with an electric mixer until is gets whiter and the texture hard.
  4. Also beat the egg yolks separatly in small bowl with na electric mixer on high speed. Gradually beat in a tablespoon of sugar and then add the melted chocolate. Finally add the egg white,
  5. Put everything in a bowl and let it cool in the fridge.
